# Fátima "Miracle of the Sun" — Eyewitness Target Matrix

**Event:** 13 October 1917, Cova da Iria, Fátima, Portugal. Crowd ~30,000–70,000+.
**Purpose:** A repo-independent catalogue of the *discrete observational claims* in the eyewitness
record, broken into "targets" we can test against the lab's atmospheric-optics models and the
agent telescope. This document deliberately makes **no commitment** to any lab hypothesis — it is the
yardstick we score the repo against in phase two.

**Method note:** Built only from primary/contemporary testimony and the published skeptical &
scientific literature (see Sources). Each target is a phenomenon *that was reported*; the confidence
column rates how well-attested the **report** is, **not** whether the cause is miraculous.

---

## Causal-layer legend

Every target is tagged with where its candidate cause would live. This is the analytic spine: the
whole debate is really *which layer each phenomenon belongs to.*

| Tag | Layer | Meaning |
|-----|-------|---------|
| **SKY** | Astronomical | A real change in/around the sun. Globally observable, photographable. (Universally rejected — no observatory recorded anything.) |
| **ATM** | Atmospheric-optical | Cause sits in the air column: clouds, aerosols, ice crystals, refraction. Photographable in principle; shareable across observers/distance. |
| **EYE** | Retinal / physiological | Cause sits in the observer's eye: bleaching, afterimages, fatigue. Per-observer; not photographable; not shared across distance. |
| **MIND** | Perceptual / social | Expectation, suggestion, autokinesis, crowd dynamics. Per-observer; not photographable. |
| **THERMAL** | Heat / evaporation | A claimed energy-transfer effect (drying, warmth). |
| **EVID** | Evidentiary | A meta-observation (consistency, distance, photos) that *discriminates* between the layers above. |

## The two hinges everything turns on

1. **T10 (distance) vs T12 (non-universality) is the master contradiction.** If the effect was genuinely
   seen ~18–40 km away, something was happening in the *air* (ATM), because retinas and crowd mood don't
   reach across a county. But if many people *at the same spot* saw nothing or saw different things, a
   single atmospheric optic can't be the whole story. The honest reading is a **layered cause**: an ATM
   trigger (dimmed, destabilized disc) that EYE/MIND effects then amplified and personalized — which is
   exactly why testimony both *converges* (silver disc, colors, motion) and *diverges* (details, some
   seeing nothing).

2. **T9 (drying) is the residue no optical model touches.** Every other target is a *light* phenomenon;
   drying is an *energy* phenomenon. It's the cleanest place to either (a) show ordinary evaporation
   suffices, or (b) concede the optical framework is incomplete. Worth isolating early.

## What this hands to phase two

Each target carries a **discriminating test** column phrased as something a physical model or the agent
telescope could actually attempt. Phase two maps the `sundog` repo's capabilities — halos, mirages,
"solar holograms," and the agent telescope — onto these tests and scores, per target:

- **Can the repo reproduce/predict the reported signature?** (yes / partial / no)
- **At which layer does the repo's mechanism sit** (ATM vs EYE), and does that match the target's most-likely layer?
- **Confidence we can defend the explanation scientifically**, separate from how well-attested the report is.

The strongest candidates for an atmospheric-optics explanation are **T1, T2, T4, T5, T13** (disc dimming,
bearable luminance, colored beams, spectral tinting, cloud-clearing trigger). The targets that resist a
pure-ATM account are **T3, T6, T7** (motion — likely EYE) and **T9** (drying — THERMAL). **T10/T12/T14**
are the evidentiary referees.
