Product Expression

Dungeon Gleaner

Indirect signal: each NPC carries unmet needs (work, socializing, errands, rest) that grow over time. Places in the dungeon — bonfires, posts, shops, benches — satisfy specific needs. The signal at any tile is the sum of how strongly the NPC's unmet needs are pulled toward nearby satisfiers.

Transformation: per tick, every reachable place is scored by combined need × proximity, with a brief pause-on-arrival and a tiny noise term to break ties. The NPC takes one greedy step toward the strongest pull.

Output: NPCs orbit organically between work, social, and errand spots. No scripted schedules. No behavior trees. No goal-oriented action planner. Personality is just per-archetype weighting on the same vocabulary of verbs.

Needs: orbit telemetry capture, side-by-side comparison against a hand-authored planner, and a tuning-sensitivity sweep.
