(HaloSim) vs C5's stated 25.4°. **Δ = 1.2° over-extension by C5.**

At C5's OUTER-wing azimuth (±25.5°): canonical radial is ≈ 25.4-26°

points sit at or beyond the canonical arc's fading limit, not on
