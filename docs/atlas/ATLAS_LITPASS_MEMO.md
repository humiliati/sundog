## 0. Why this lane needs a lit-pass at all

The atlas invents **no** physics. Its apparatus, its geometry, and its catastrophe-theoretic skeleton
are entirely borrowed; the Sundog contribution is the *synthesis* (forward-generated complete atlas as
a **classified bifurcation diagram** + the determining-shadow-set thesis embodiment + the directed
invisible-halo search). A synthesis lane stands or falls on attribution discipline and on not
re-deriving (or misattributing) known results. The public workbench already has a flagged
above-the-fold attribution gap (`docs/calibration/PHASE11_OUTREACH_SYNTHETIC_MEMO.md` W2); this memo
is the instrument that closes it before any tier-3 outreach.

## 1. Tracks (what the pass must settle)

- **Track A — Halo geometry & simulation (the body).** The ray-optics theory of halos and the
  Monte-Carlo simulators. Establish exactly what is canonical, what is each author's, and what the
  apparatus (HaloSim) already computes, so Sundog claims nothing already standard as novel.
- **Track B — Catastrophe / caustic optics (the skeleton).** The classification of caustics and their
  diffraction dressing. Establish that "a halo is a fold," "merges are cusps," and "the diffraction
  fringe encodes scale" are *known structure*, and isolate what (if anything) about the **bifurcation
  set of the halo control space (elevation × habit × orientation)** is not already explicitly written.
- **Track C — The two-component wall taxonomy.** Confirm the literature's treatment of (A) caustic
  catastrophes vs (B) ray-admissibility / TIR boundaries, and whether the *union* is named as the
  organizing structure of the complete atlas anywhere.
- **Track D — Determinacy / inverse problem.** Prior art on the halo inverse problem (recovering
  crystal populations from a display) and on what a display *does* and *does not* determine — the
  determining-shadow-set framing in optics terms.
- **Track E — Crystal size from diffraction (Phase 8.5).** Prior art on reading particle size off
  diffraction structure (rainbow supernumeraries → drop size is textbook; the halo / ice-crystal
  analogue and its limits).
- **Track F — Attribution & priority map.** A per-claim ledger: for every statement the atlas will
  make, name the source it rests on, and flag any that read as a Sundog priority claim for demotion.

## 3. Gap map (what the pass is looking for)

The pass must place each item below into one of: **STANDARD** (already in the literature — credit,
don't claim), **SYNTHESIS** (Sundog's recombination of standard parts — defensible as framing), or
**OPEN** (genuinely unaddressed — candidate contribution, still bounded).

1. "A halo is a fold caustic (minimum deviation)." — expected **STANDARD** (Berry/Nye).
2. "The merge/appear/disappear transitions form the bifurcation set of the halo control space." —
   expected **SYNTHESIS** at minimum; check whether anyone states it as the organizing skeleton of the
   *complete* atlas (vs. per-phenomenon).
3. "Two-component wall taxonomy: caustic catastrophes (A) ∪ ray-admissibility boundaries (B)." — check
   whether the union is named; expected **SYNTHESIS/OPEN**.
4. "The bifurcation set is the determining shadow of the atlas topology (read transitions off it, can't
   rebuild the display)." — the cross-substrate framing; expected **SYNTHESIS** (Sundog vocabulary over
   standard non-invertibility).
5. "Invisible halos live at the higher-codimension strata (swallowtail/umbilic)." — check prior
   predictions of rare halos via higher catastrophes (Können/Tape may have geometric versions);
   expected **STANDARD-or-SYNTHESIS** — the *directed forward search* is the Sundog move.
6. "Crystal size readable off the diffraction dressing of a halo fold." — Track E; expected
   **STANDARD for rainbows, OPEN/bounded for ice halos**.

## 4. Disposition (precondition for the lane)

- **Attribution requirement (blocking).** Before any tier-3 outreach or public copy: the apparatus
  (Cowley & Schroeder) and the geometry (Greenler / Tape / Können / Cowley) must be credited
  prominently and above-the-fold on the public surfaces (closes
  `PHASE11_OUTREACH_SYNTHETIC_MEMO.md` W2); catastrophe optics (Thom / Arnold / Berry / Nye) credited
  wherever the bifurcation framing appears.
- **No-priority rule.** Every roadmap claim carries its Track-F source; anything that reads as priority
  over the giants is demoted to "we apply / synthesize."
- **Bounded-novelty rule.** The defensible novelty is the *synthesis* (classified-bifurcation-diagram
  organization of the forward-generated atlas + the determinacy framing + the directed invisible-halo
  search), never the underlying physics.
